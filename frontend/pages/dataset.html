Dataset profile

Code-mixed corpus designed for entity-rich informal text

CogniTag targets the challenging space of Roman Urdu + English code-mixed user text, where spelling variation, transliteration drift, and informal syntax make named entity recognition difficult.

Challenges
  • Transliteration variance
    The same Urdu word may appear in multiple Roman spellings.
  • Informal syntax
    Chat-like structure and social text reduce the usefulness of rigid grammar assumptions.
  • Mixed capitalization
    Many user-written sentences do not preserve proper noun capitalization, which affects entity cues.
  • Entity ambiguity
    Names, organizations, and locations often overlap in informal short sentences.
Example sentence

"ali kal karachi gaya tha aur microsoft ke office me interview diya"

Ali → PER Karachi → LOC Microsoft → ORG
Training implication

Because the task is code-mixed and informal, a multilingual transformer like XLM-R is a better deployment match than handcrafted pipelines. Your current frontend/backend design reflects that stronger research story.
